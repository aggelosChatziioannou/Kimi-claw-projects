Who We Are

Greek Coffee started in 2015 with a dream: to bring authentic Greek coffee to a modern, welcoming environment in Ioannina.

We believe that coffee is not just a drink - it is an experience. It is the moment that starts your day, the excuse to meet friends, the reason to take a break from work.

At Greek Coffee, every cup is a promise of quality. We use only selected beans, roasted in the traditional Greek way, and served with respect to tradition.
